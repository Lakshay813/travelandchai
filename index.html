Must-See Places

Pangot / Haryal

Feed all your adventure cravings here, situated at the top of a hill, 17kms from Nainital, away from the madness of the cities. Located inside the Naina Devi Bird Conservation Reserve, to reach the location one has to trek 500mts, we offer several adventure activities, from bird watching, nature walks to jungle safari, we also organise treks and offer local cuisine in a traditional setting.

Vashisht

Vashisht is a small village located around 3 km from Manali across the River Beas. This beautiful village is famous for its sulphurous hot water springs and Vashisht temple, built just next to the springs.

Mana Village

Mana is a last Indian village from the Indo-China border. It is located in the district of Chamoli. It has been designated as a Tourism Village by the Uttarakhand
